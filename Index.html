About

Mogomotsi², born and bred in Pretoria in the Gauteng province of South Africa, is an aspiring Photographer who discovered his love for photography at the age of 11 and developed his digital art skills in order to pursue his career in game development. Mogomotsi² is also a qualified systems developer and is currently completing his Bachelor of Arts in Visual Multimedia Arts at the University of South Africa.
